# Instructions

## How to Add a Custom Command in Twitch Using Nightbot

Twitch chat bots allow streamers to automate responses and create interactive commands for their viewers. One of the most popular bots is Nightbot, which allows users to create custom commands that respond when viewers type specific commands in chat.

---

## Steps

### Step 1: Open Nightbot
Go to the Nightbot website: https://nightbot.tv  

### Step 2: Add Nightbot to Your Chat
Click the **“Add to Chat”** button in the top right corner.
<img width="1236" height="631" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b2529c9e-3be1-412e-8aa9-a7e8d94e38ef" />

*Figure 1. Nightbot homepage. (Source: https://nightbot.tv)*

---

### Step 3: Sign in with Twitch
Click **“Sign in with Twitch”** to connect your Twitch account.

### Step 4: Authorize Nightbot
Click **“Authorize”** when prompted to allow Nightbot access to your account.
<img width="536" height="875" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b832b4ec-f5e8-42ad-b193-17e986b5a52a" />

*Figure 2. Authorization screen. (Source: https://nightbot.tv)*

---

### Step 5: Open Commands Menu
In the Nightbot dashboard, click **“Commands”** in the left sidebar.

### Step 6: Select Custom Commands
Click **“Custom”** from the commands menu.

### Step 7: Add a New Command
Click the **“Add Command”** button in the upper right corner.

---

### Step 8: Create Your Command
- In the **Command** field, type your command (example: `!hello`)  
- In the **Message** field, type the response Nightbot will send
